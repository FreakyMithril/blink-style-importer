# Privacy Policy — Blink Style Importer

_Last updated: July 2026 (version 1.0.0)_

Blink Style Importer is a Chrome extension that lets you write custom CSS for websites and applies it automatically when you visit them.

## What data the extension handles

- **Your custom CSS** and **editor preferences** (autosave on/off, autosave delay, editor font size) are stored in `chrome.storage.sync`, keyed by website hostname. This storage belongs to your own Chrome profile and is synced across your devices by your Google account, exactly like other Chrome settings.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does not collect, transmit, or share any data with the developer or any third party.
- It does not read or record page content, browsing history, keystrokes, or any personal information.
- It contains no analytics, tracking, advertising, or remote code.
- It makes no network requests.

## Data removal

Use the "Clear site" / "Clear all…" buttons in the extension popup to delete stored styles, or remove the extension — Chrome then deletes its storage.
